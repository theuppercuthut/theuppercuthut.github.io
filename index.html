Our Classes

Did you know that boxing has proven weight loss benefits? It’s not only fun to learn, but it’s something that you can practice and apply at home when you’re not at your scheduled boxing classes.

The Uppercut hut enables you to get fit but not hit. We are a boxing-related fitness club that helps to put fun back into training in the comfort of our clean and immaculate gym facilities.

The Uppercut hut offers boxing classes for everyone. You can join at any level or time in your life. There is no experience required to start. We offer a friendly group session atmosphere, but if you prefer, we also offer personal training on request. Our team consists of dedicated and experienced boxing trainers. Our group sessions range from small to large groups.

We offer boxing classes to all men, women, and children. Only on Fridays 6pm till 8pm

Please join us for one of the following classes.

£7.50 per lesson, £2.50 of which will go to Velindre Cancer Centre

Phone us on 07399815762

Email us at theuppercut@icloud.com

The Uppercut hut Juniors

The Uppercut hut Juniors offers boxing classes to children from the ages of 8 years and up to 18 years old. Only 6pm to 7pm. It trains children in discipline, confidence, and self-defence so they’re better prepared for life.

Boxing Small Groups

Some people prefer to learn in smaller groups so they get the individualised attention they desire. These classes are also helpful for beginners who need a bit of extra guidance. If you’re not certain whether a larger class is right for you, please ask to have a taster of one of our smaller group classes and see if they are better for you.

1 to 1 Personal Training

Some people prefer to learn 1 to 1 so they get the individualised attention they desire. Please contact us regarding 1 to 1 sessions.
